WHAT IS REACH OUT?

Tap into the world's largest network of licensed, accredited, and experienced therapists who can help you with a range of issues including depression, anxiety, relationships, trauma, grief, and more. With our therapists, you get the same professionalism and quality you would expect from an in-office therapist, but with the ability to communicate when and how you want.

Message your therapist anytime from anywhere. No scheduling needed. Schedule a live session at a time that's convenient for you. Connect from your phone, tablet, or computer. Talk to your therapist however you feel comfortable.

Users can connect with counselors to understand and help you with your mental health problems.

Be a counselor and offer your expertise to help others cope with mental health problems.

Counselors can assess the conversation easier using a conversation AI tool.

ABOUT US

We are passionate and compassionate professionals, driven by the mission of helping more people live a better and happier life every day. We are growing fast and always looking for new talent. If you love people and like challenges - come and join us!
